# Project Brief: INFINITE GUNDAWAR BUSINESS PRIVATE LIMITED - Marketing Web App

## Company Details
- **Company Name:** INFINITE GUNDAWAR BUSINESS PRIVATE LIMITED
- **Registered in:** Maharashtra, India
- **Share Capital:** Rs. 1,00,000 (10,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each)
- **Director:** NIRAJ SUNILRAO GUNDAWAR (Gadchiroli, Maharashtra)

## Business Areas
1. **Infrastructure & Real Estate** - Infrastructure developers, builders, promoters, contractors, town planners, surveyors, property agents
2. **Import/Export Trading** - Consumer goods, electronics, industrial products, agricultural products, technology products, fashion & lifestyle
3. **Coaching & Educational Services** - Skill development, professional certification, competitive exam prep, coaching classes
4. **Digital Marketing & Advertising** - Marketing campaigns, branding, promotional activities

## Web App Requirements

### Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14+ (React) with TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Next.js API routes (full-stack)
- Database can be added later (keep it static/SSG-first)

### Pages & Sections Needed
1. **Home/Hero** - Company tagline, key highlights, CTA
2. **About Us** - Company overview, history, vision, mission
3. **Services (split into 4 sections):**
   - Infrastructure & Real Estate Development
   - Import/Export Trading
   - Coaching & Educational Services
   - Digital Marketing & Advertising
4. **Portfolio/Projects** - Showcase completed or ongoing projects
5. **Testimonials** - Client/f Customer testimonials
6. **Contact Us** - Contact form, address, phone, email, Google Maps embed
7. **Team/Leadership** - Director and key people

### Design Preferences
- Modern, professional, corporate look
- Clean and minimal with bold accent colors
- Responsive (mobile-first)
- Indian corporate aesthetic with international appeal

### Additional Features
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Animated sections on scroll (subtle animations)
- SEO optimized (meta tags, structured data)
- Fast loading, good performance scores

## Notes
- This is the FIRST version - keep it clean and professional
- Use placeholder images where needed (unsplash or placeholder.com)
- All content should reflect the company's diverse business areas
- The contact form can be a frontend-only form (Formspree or similar) for now
